Smart Fish Feeding and Water Quality Monitoring System

This project helps you easily take care of your fish by monitoring water quality and feeding them with just a tap on your phone.

Key Features

  • Real-time water quality monitoring
  • Remote feeding control
  • Easy to use and fully automated

How It Works

The system includes sensors, an Arduino, and a mobile app that work together to make fish care easy.
